Membership Level

NP Membership (Non-Profit)

Cost: Free
Are you a non-profit organization dedicated to making a positive impact in Caracas? Our NP Membership is tailored just for you. Enjoy all the networking and support benefits of our Chamber without any membership fees. Connect with other like-minded organizations, access valuable resources, and participate in exclusive events designed to help you achieve your charitable goals.

Bronze Membership

Cost: $199/year
Our Bronze Membership is the perfect choice for businesses and organizations looking to establish a solid foundation in the Caracas business community. With this level, you gain access to a wide range of benefits including business training, marketing opportunities, and special discounts on Chamber events. Showcase your commitment to growth and success with the Bronze Membership.

Silver Membership

Cost: $499/year
Elevate your presence in the Caracas business landscape with our Silver Membership. Enjoy all the advantages of the Bronze Membership plus additional perks such as prominent advertising placements on our website, exclusive invitations to high-profile events, and personalized support from our Chamber team. Take your business to the next level with the Silver Membership.

Gold Membership

Cost: $999/year
For those who demand the utmost from their Chamber of Commerce experience, our Gold Membership is the pinnacle of membership levels. With this elite status, you gain unparalleled exposure through prime advertising positions, complimentary attendance at premium events, personalized business consultations, and top-tier networking opportunities. Invest in your success with the Gold Membership and position your business at the forefront of Caracas commerce.
